Facts About Boba

Boba tea (also known as bubble tea or pearl milk tea) originated in Taiwan during the 1980s. It was created by combining shaken iced tea with milk and chewy tapioca pearls, quickly becoming a popular cultural staple that has since spread worldwide.

It Was First Served as Iced Tea. Bubble tea was originally a mixture of cold black tea, tapioca pearls, and sugar. Later, milk and fruit flavors were added, sparking a wide variety of creative combinations that make bubble tea so beloved today.
